Welcome to the BS IT Program

Are you passionate about technology and innovation? Are you eager to make a mark in the digital world? Our BSIT program is uniquely designed to empower you with the skills and knowledge necessary to excel in the ever-evolving field of Information Technology. Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) offers a dynamic and future-proof career path in a world increasingly driven by technology.By studying BSIT, you'll gain expertise in areas such as software development, network security, database management, and system administration. Explore the website for more information.

Program Goals

Quality Education: Provide a high-quality education that balances theoretical knowledge with practical, hands-on experience.
Industry Relevance: Ensure that the curriculum remains relevant to current and future industry needs, incorporating the latest technological advancements and trends.
Research and Innovation: Promote a culture of research and innovation, encouraging students to engage in projects that contribute to the advancement of IT knowledge and practice.
Ethical Responsibility: Emphasize the importance of ethics in technology, teaching students to consider the societal impact of their work and to use their skills responsibly.
Career Readiness: Equip graduates with the tools and confidence needed to excel in IT careers, including the ability to work effectively in teams, communicate clearly, and lead projects.
Community Engagement: Foster a sense of community engagement and responsibility, encouraging students to contribute to societal development through their IT expertise.

Program Objectives

Technical Proficiency: Equip students with in-depth knowledge and skills in IT fundamentals, including programming, systems analysis, networking, and cybersecurity.
Problem-Solving Skills: Develop students’ abilities to identify, analyze, and solve complex IT problems through critical thinking and innovative approaches.
Professional Preparedness: Prepare graduates for diverse career paths in the IT industry by fostering strong communication skills, ethical practices, and teamwork.
Adaptability and Lifelong Learning: Instill a commitment to continuous learning and adaptability in an ever-evolving technological landscape.
Global Perspective: Encourage a global outlook on IT trends, practices, and challenges, promoting an understanding of cultural and international diversity in technology.

ISU Vision, Mission and Quality Objectives

VISION
A leading Research University in the ASEAN Region.
MISSION
The Isabela State University is committed to develop globally competitive human, technological resources and services through quality instruction, innovative research, responsive community engagement and viable resource management programs for inclusive growth and sustainable development.
Quality Policy
The ISABELA STATE UNIVERSITY endeavors to be a lead University in instruction, research, extension and resource generation, through continual improvement of services and commits to comply with the standards set by statutory, regulatory and accrediting bodies.
To uphold the commitment, ISU shall attain the following quality objectives:
1. Sustain academic excellence and quality in instruction;
2. Generate research breakthroughs;
3. Engage in sectoral activities for community development;
4. Develop products for glocalization;
5. Support students participation to local and international fora to enhance their potentialities; and
6. Review on periodic basis, the Quality Management System (QMS) and gather feedback on the level of client satisfaction as basis for continuous improvement.
